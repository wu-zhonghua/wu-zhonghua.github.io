Zhonghua Wu (吴仲华)

Zhonghua Wu is currently a third year PhD student at Nanyang Technological Unversity, supervised by Prof. Lin Guosheng and Prof. Cai Jianfei. Previously, he recieved a bachlor degree at College of Electronic Engineering (EE) at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2017.

His research interests are broadly in computer vision, including image generation, image detection and segmentation, point cloud segmentation, etc.
