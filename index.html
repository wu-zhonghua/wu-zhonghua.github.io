About Me

Zhonghua Wu is currently a Researcher at SenseTime International. Prior to that, he completed his PhD at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological Unversity, supervised by Prof. Lin Guosheng and Prof. Cai Jianfei. Previously, he recieved a bachlor degree from College of Electronic Engineering (EE) at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

His research interests are broadly in computer vision, with a focus on multimodal large language models (MLLM), unified understanding and generation models, AI-generated content (AIGC), image restoration, visual understanding etc.
